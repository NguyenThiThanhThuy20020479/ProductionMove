Services

In order to bring the best experience to customers, VinFast constantly improves service quality and invests in synchronous equipment system. All VinFast service workshops are fully equipped with technical machinery according to European standards.

Customer Care

VinFast owns a system of 64 showrooms nationwide, so customers will receive a lot of support services after buying a car.

Warranty and maintenance policy

With a new car, customers will be warranted, replacing parts according to the company's policy if there is a warranty period.

Battery exchange service

Most of VinFast's electric motorcycles use lithium batteries, which are extremely safe, convenient and environmentally friendly.
